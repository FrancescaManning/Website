The violin

When I was seven years old, we received a letter in the mail from Kulturskolan asking us if we wanted to try out an instrument. So, that fall we went to the school to try out instruments. My sister and I tried out all the instruments but the one I though was most fun was the violin. Since then I have played for eight and a half years and really enjoy it. It has taught me to perservere, practice and have patience. Learning to play an instrument can be helpful in these ways since you have to practice to gain skills. Many people quit as a kid and then regret it by the time they grow up. I am glad I decided to continue.
